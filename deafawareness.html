Deaf Awareness

Explore journey into the 'silent world' with our local deaf artists, powerful movies like 'The Silent Child' and 'Sound of Metal', along with inspiring artists like Sean Forbes and Mandy Harvey, to understand the challenges of deaf culture, break communication barriers, and promote inclusivity and understanding through music and film.
