Clarice Victoria Zi Wang SHIM

Student ID 102788227
Course Bachelor of Computer Science
Demographic

Hi! My name is Clarice and my favourite colours are pink and bling, though one could argue bling isn’t an actual colour and I would agree but I love anything in the colour shiny, so I rest my case. I am mostly Chinese with a hint of Iban and Melanau somewhere in my blood, but I mainly speak English as I was brought up with the language. I’m extroverted in nature and enjoy spending time with people, but I do find myself seeking downtime alone instead these days. I’m always trying to better myself and thus I always try my best with any work that I have. However, I am also working part-time whilst studying so I might have some work delayed due to time and energy constraints, for this I ask for some understand. I hope to meet good friends during my university life ^^.

Hometown

I grew up in Kuching, the capital city of Sarawak. It’s rich with diversity and culture, mixed with a sprinkle of modernity. The majority speaks Hokkien alongside Malay, Mandarin or English.
Greatest achievement

My greatest achievement so far is staying committed to my responsibilities. It was hard all throughout high school for me to stay remotely committed to any activity, let alone a big responsibility. I am proud to say I’ve grown since.
