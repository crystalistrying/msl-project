Charity Car Wash

Deaf Individuals Deliver Exceptional Charity Car Wash Services

Get ready to make a splash at our charity car wash event, where every scrub and rinse goes towards a great cause. Join us for a hands-on experience, guided step by step by our dedicated team, including members from the deaf community. Together, we will ensure that your car sparkles while making a meaningful difference in the lives of others.

Your involvement in our car wash event directly benefits the Sarawak Society for the Deaf (SSD), providing essential resources and support to those in need. As interest in sign language grows, so does the impact of our fundraising efforts. Your participation serves as a 'sign' of solidarity, reminding the deaf community that they are valued members of our society.

Participants not only leave with a sparkling clean car but also with a sense of fulfillment, knowing they have contributed to a worthy cause. Whether you are seeking a spotless ride or simply want to make a difference, our charity car wash event offers the perfect opportunity to do both. Join us and be a part of something special!

Why a car wash?

Holding a charity car wash is one of the many jobs our deaf members can participate in to get some funding.

Despite being unable to participate in environments that heavily depend on verbal communication, our deaf members have the full ability to perform as well as the hearing when it comes to task-focused jobs.

A car wash pre-assigns tasks and require minimal communication in between washes, helping the Deaf put in all focus into their job.

Now, not only do they get to build confidence in competency, they also get to keep an active lifestyle all while spreading awareness about the Sarawak Society for the Deaf through this charity car wash!
