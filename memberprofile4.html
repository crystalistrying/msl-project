Crystal Sing Yi ONG

Student ID 102788366
Course Bachelor of Computer Science
Demographic

My name is Crystal and I am 19 years old. I am introverted and I enjoy spending my time either alone or with a select group of people. In terms of academics, I strive to do well and I have a bad habit of getting too competitive and comparative with others around me. When I get stressed, I tend to forget those around me and lose myself in my worries, however, most days I try to be as friendly and as kind as I can be to people I meet.
Hometown

Surrounded by the stunning landscapes of Borneo, my hometown is a vibrant blend of cultures and flavours. From its bustling streets to serene riverbanks, Kuching offers a unique mix of modernity and tradition. The city's rich culinary scene, colourful history, and close connection to nature make it a place I'll always cherish.
Greatest achievement

The greatest achievement in my life so far is getting good results for my SPM. I studied tirelessly for months to get the results that I wanted as it has always been a childhood dream to do well.
