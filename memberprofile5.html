Demographic

My name is Wee Wee and at nineteen years old. I find great joy in video editing, relishing the creative process of shaping raw footage into a compelling story. At the same time, I love the moments spent with my friends, this enriches my life.
Hometown

My hometown, Miri is a coastal city in northeastern Sarawak, Malaysia, located near the border of Brunei. It's the second largest city in Sarawak and is known for its vibrant nightlife, beautiful beaches, and as a gateway to several national parks and nature reserves.
Greatest achievement

For me, my biggest achievement is my artwork has been chosen as one of the collections of "Through Our Eyes" artbook. This is a book that was made for Jennie from the K-pop group Blackpink on her birthday in 2021s.
