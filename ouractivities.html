Upcoming Activities

Charity Food Fair

Come support us at our Charity Food Fair for the deaf !

We as MSL, we are calling for your attention to increase awareness, inclusivity, and support the deaf individuals and communities and you can start by participating in our upcoming bazaar, organized by the sarawak society of the deaf aimed to showcase their talents and raise awareness. Spread the word to all friends and family to help promote inclusivity and equality for the deaf community!

Date: 7th April 2024
Time: 8am - 12.30pm
Place: Association of Churches in Sarawak

What can you do to help the cause?

  1. Set up booths and participate in selling your favourite foods!
  2. Purchase and enjoy all good foods available for sale
  3. Join our activities planned for the day
